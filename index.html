3. Nanotechnology: Precision Treatment Without Side Effects

By using nanobots, tiny programmed particles injected into the bloodstream, doctors can now target and destroy individual cancer cells without harming surrounding healthy tissue. This eliminates the painful side effects of chemotherapy and radiation while ensuring that no cancerous cell is left behind.
